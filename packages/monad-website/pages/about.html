Monad is a tiny SSG that stays out of your way.

You write HTML templates, compose them with partials, and let Vite bundle assets. Monad stitches it into a clean static site, then runs an audit so you don’t forget the basics.

Philosophy

  • HTML-first: templates you can reason about.
  • Composable: reuse sections like Lego bricks.
  • Marketing-ready: sitemap, robots, metadata, and audits.
  • Optional power: OG images + Lighthouse CI when you want it.
